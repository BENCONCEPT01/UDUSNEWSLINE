UDUS Admission 2024/2025 Academic Session: Confirmation of Admission Ongoing

Published on January 3, 2025

The management of Usmanu Danfodiyo University, Sokoto (UDUS) has officially announced that the confirmation of admission for the 2024/2025 academic session is currently ongoing. All candidates who participated in the admission process are encouraged to verify and confirm their admission status to proceed with the necessary steps for registration.

Important Details for Candidates:

Admission Confirmation:
Candidates who applied for admission into various undergraduate programs at UDUS are advised to check the official portal for confirmation of their admission status. Ensure that you have your application number and other relevant details readily available to check your admission status.

Action Required:
If your admission is confirmed, follow the outlined instructions to proceed with registration. Candidates whose admissions are not confirmed should await further instructions or take the necessary steps as per the university's guidance.

Registration Process:
After confirming your admission, you will be required to complete the registration process, including paying any required fees, filling out necessary forms, and providing other relevant documentation. Timely registration is crucial to secure your place in the upcoming academic session.

Deadline for Confirmation:
Candidates are advised to complete the confirmation process as soon as possible. Any delays may affect your ability to register for the new session.
